SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into as of July 1, 2026, by and between CloudSolutions Corp., a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of New York (hereinafter referred to as the "Service Provider"), and RetailChain Inc., a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of Texas (hereinafter referred to as the "Client").

WHEREAS, the Service Provider desires to provide certain services to the Client; and

WHEREAS, the Client desires to engage the Service Provider to provide such services under the terms and conditions set forth herein.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
The Service Provider agrees to provide the following services (hereinafter referred to as the "Services"):
a) Cloud infrastructure management and maintenance
b) 24/7 technical support and monitoring
c) Regular system backups and disaster recovery planning
d) Security updates and patch management
e) Performance optimization and reporting

2. TERM
This Agreement shall commence on July 1, 2026, and shall continue for a period of one (1) year, unless earlier terminated by either party in accordance with the provisions of Section 8. Upon expiration, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the expiration date.

3. PAYMENT TERMS
a) The Client agrees to pay the Service Provider a monthly fee of $5,000.00 for the Services provided.
b) Invoices shall be issued on the first day of each month and payment shall be due within thirty (30) days of invoice date.
c) Late payments shall accrue interest at the rate of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate allowed by law, whichever is less.
d) All fees are exclusive of applicable sales taxes, which shall be borne by the Client.

4. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
a) The Service Provider shall maintain a system uptime of at least 99.5% per month, measured from 12:00 AM to 11:59 PM Eastern Time.
b) Scheduled maintenance windows, not to exceed 4 hours per month, shall be excluded from uptime calculations.
c) In the event of failure to meet the SLA, the Client shall be entitled to service credits as follows:
   - 99.0% to 99.4% uptime: 5% monthly fee credit
   - 98.0% to 98.9% uptime: 10% monthly fee credit
   - Below 98.0% uptime: 25% monthly fee credit

5. RESPONSIBILITIES
a) Service Provider Responsibilities:
   i) Provide timely and professional Services as described in Section 1
   ii) Maintain appropriate security measures to protect Client data
   iii) Provide monthly performance reports
   iv) Respond to support requests within 4 business hours for critical issues

b) Client Responsibilities:
   i) Provide accurate information necessary for Service delivery
   ii) Maintain appropriate access controls and user management
   iii) Pay all fees in accordance with Section 3
   iv) Notify Service Provider of any issues or concerns within 24 hours

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of any proprietary or sensitive information disclosed by the other party, except as required by law or with prior written consent.

7. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION
a) Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages arising out of this Agreement.
b) The Service Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid by the Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.
c) The Client shall indemnify and hold harmless the Service Provider from any claims arising from the Client's use of the Services in violation of applicable law.

8. TERMINATION
a) Either party may terminate this Agreement for cause if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days of written notice.
b) The Client may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon ninety (90) days' written notice.
c) Upon termination, the Service Provider shall provide the Client with all data in a standard format within thirty (30) days.

9. RENEWAL AND RATE ADJUSTMENTS
a) This Agreement shall automatically renew unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to expiration.
b) Upon renewal, the Service Provider may increase fees by up to 5% annually, with thirty (30) days' written notice.

10. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

11. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement, together with any exhibits attached hereto, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements.
